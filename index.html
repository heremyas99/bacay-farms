Meet the Founder

From a Simple Farmer's Dream
to Your Table

Early Years · 2010

Jeremie Bacay was born in Bagong Buhay, Naujan, to a family of rice farmers. Growing up, he woke before sunrise to tend the fields alongside his father, learning that soil and patience are the two greatest ingredients of all.

The Turning Point · 2013

After studying Information Technology at Mindoro State University, Jeremie returned home with one goal: prove that small, local farms could feed communities better than any grocery chain. He converted three hectares of inherited land into a diverse vegetable farm.

Opening Day · 2015

Bacay Farms Restaurant opened its doors with just five menu items and a handful of plastic tables under a bamboo roof. Within months, word spread across the city about the freshest, most honest food in Naujan and the humble farm became a local landmark.

Today · Growing Strong

Now serving hundreds of happy guests daily, Bacay Farms employs 12 full-time farmers and kitchen staff, all from the local barangay. Jeremie still walks the fields every morning before the kitchen opens, picking the day's harvest by hand.
